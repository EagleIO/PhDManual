Chapter 1 I/O Psychology Doctoral Program Roles and Policies

As the I/O Doctoral Program goes live, we are in need of a handbook detailing all the key procedures and policies for the I/O doctoral and MA program experience. The goal is to have a final draft of the handbook by December 1st 2020, or the deadline for our first official doctoral application cycle. We need to have formal procedures for the following areas: (1) Doctoral Student Selection, Chapter ??, (2) I/O Program (MA and PhD) Probation Standards, Chapter 2, (3) Prospective Comprehensive Exams, Chapter 3, (4) Student Mentorship and Program Culture, Chapter 4, and (5) Dissertation Process, Chapter 5.
